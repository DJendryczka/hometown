Top three activities to do in Malmö

Malmö Festival

Welcome to The Malmö Festival - a warm, friendly and wide-ranging festival founded in 1985

Oresund Bridge

The magnificent Oresund Bridge (Øresundsbroen) lies a 15-minute drive from Malmö city center.

Explore Malmö Castle

A must-see for any visitor, Malmö Castle (Malmöhus) houses several of the city's major museums.
